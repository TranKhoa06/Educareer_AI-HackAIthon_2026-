\# EduCareer AI Roadmap



This roadmap describes the current development direction of EduCareer AI.



The roadmap may change based on technical requirements, educational review, community feedback, and user testing.



\## Completed



\- Initial public web prototype.

\- Academic transcript OCR workflow.

\- Academic profile processing.

\- RIASEC career-interest assessment.

\- Personality-related assessments.

\- Personal competency map.

\- AI-assisted career counseling prototype.

\- Vietnamese career knowledge base.

\- Initial career simulation activities.

\- Public deployment.



\## Current Priorities



\- Improve project documentation.

\- Improve source-code organization.

\- Improve mobile responsiveness.

\- Improve accessibility.

\- Improve input validation.

\- Improve error handling.

\- Expand automated testing.

\- Review privacy and security practices.

\- Review AI prompts.

\- Improve AI recommendation transparency.

\- Expand career and educational information.

Future development may include:

\- Feedback tools for students and teachers.

\## Long-Term Vision



Our long-term goal is to develop EduCareer AI into an accessible, transparent, responsible, and community-driven educational platform for Vietnamese students.



EduCareer AI aims to help students better understand their strengths, interests, abilities, and possible career pathways.



The platform is designed to support human decision-making rather than replace teachers, parents, career counselors, educational institutions, or professional guidance.
